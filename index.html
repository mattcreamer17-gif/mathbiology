How we think about it

Sixty percent of any AI product is traditional code and data work. Thirty percent is rule-based logic. Ten percent is genuine AI.

Teams often over-weight the AI slice in their narrative and under-document the other seventy. For a device like DMA, the governance story is the seventy. The AI slice gets cleaner the moment the documentation layer is honest about where the model ends and the rules and the deterministic code begin.

60%
Traditional code & data
Pipelines, databases, ETL, application logic. Documented like software, tested like software.
30%
Rule-based logic
Deterministic protocols, thresholds, decision tables. Auditable. Readable by a regulator without a data scientist in the room.
10%
Genuine AI
Where a model is actually earning its keep. Documented with model cards, provenance, and validation. This is the slice regulators press on.
